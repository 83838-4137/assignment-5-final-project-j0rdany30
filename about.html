    ABOUT

    Discover the story behind 876 Bar and Grill, where tradition meets innovation and every guest becomes part of our Jamaican culinary journey.

    MESSAGE

    Born and raised in Jamaica, I'm steeped in a culture renowned for its culinary and drink traditions. Jerk cooking, a Jamaican specialty involving a spicy rub or marinade, and the world-famous Jamaican rums like the century-old Appleton, are staples of our cuisine. In my family, holidays are celebrated with gatherings featuring Jerk Chicken and Pork, accompanied by rum-based beverages. Inspired by this rich heritage and family customs, I founded 876 Bar and Grill to share these unique, authentic experiences.

    Jordan Yeo, Founder

    MISSION

    Our mission at 876 is to present a re-imagined authentic Jamaican grilling and drinking experience through a contemporary lens inspired by family traditions. 876 offers a unique experience by not only serving authentic contemporary Jamaican cuisine and drinks but will also follow traditional Jamaican bar/grill practices. Its setting will capture a modern-rustic jerk center, that grills outdoors on a bed of sweetwood and pimento sticks, covered with large sheets of corrugated tin. Its bar provides a broad selection of Jamaica's finest rum spirits and the entertainment covers a wide array of Jamaican music genres both live and pre-recorded.

    PHILOSOPHY

    Most Jamaican or Caribbean restaurants also create generic experiences that serve unimaginative cuisine. The Jamaican national motto is 'Out of Many One People' referring to the population's multicultural roots. Jamaican cuisine is a product of this melting pot. At 876 we aim to give customers, who are of the age of 21 and over, from all economic, cultural, and racial backgrounds a fresh modern take on what it means and feels like to be a Jamaican.
